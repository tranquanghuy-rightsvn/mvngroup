Latest Awards

1st Prize winner of the Design Competition of
Quy Nhon City Square

The People’s Committee of Bình Định Province has recently announced the results of the “Design & Planning Competition for the Quy Nhơn City Square” in April 2025.

Taking the top honor, the winning design "A Shining Gem Amidst the Flow of History" by MVN Ggroup Company Limited - PA8888 impressed the jury with its bold creativity, strong feasibility, outstanding optimization of public space, and an appealing investment efficiency index. Brimming with potential, the project is envisioned to become a striking architectural landmark — a seamless fusion of art, the rich cultural heritage of the region, and a vibrant spirit of community connection.
